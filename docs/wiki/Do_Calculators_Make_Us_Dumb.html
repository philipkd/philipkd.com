Do Calculators Make Us Dumb

Andrew Ballinger on Quora:

If it takes less time to use a calculator then it makes you smarter. If it takes you more time (and for simple arithmetic it often will) it makes you dumber.

If someone asks a group of people hard arithmetic problems, the ones without calculators are going to take longer and get wrong answers, sounds pretty dumb to me.

If someone asks a group of people easy arithmetic problems, the ones that have to get out their calculators and type are going to be the slow, dumb ones.

The really dumb thing is not knowing when to use a calculator.

This is a functional definition of being smart or proficient. Calculator-use becomes more favored that mental-times-tables. Likewise, the ability to manipulate interfaces becomes more favored, and alas it's computer programmers today who are rewarded with high salaries, not the math whiz in high school.
