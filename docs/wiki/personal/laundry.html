High-Efficiency detergent

HE detergent generates fewer suds and HE washing machines use less water. The proper suds-to-water ration is important. As a result:

  • HE detergent in a non-HE washing machine requires more detergent
  • non-HE detergent in a HE washing machine requires less detergent

Note: Energy Star does not imply HE automatically
